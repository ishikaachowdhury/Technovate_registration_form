HACKAMANIA

Technovate, the first ever tech fest of IEM, Ashram is going to organise Hackamania, A 12-hour hackathon. featuring 25 teams of 4 members each. Participants will compete across five exciting themes: Open Source, AI & Machine Learning ,IOT, Cyber Security, E-commerce. This event promises to be a platform for innovation, where students can collaborate and develop solutions to real-world problems. Hackamania aims to ignite creativity and foster technical skills, making it a landmark event for aspiring tech enthusiasts.Winners will get exciting prizes,goodies etc...

DARK SYNTAX

Join us for Dark Syntax, a thrilling coding competition that will put your skills to the ultimate test! Participants will face a series of challenging coding problems, and the first three coders to finish will take home exciting prizes. With a focus on quick thinking and problem-solving, this event promises to be both intense and rewarding. You might be thinking that why it is called DARK Syntax? well, to find out join us for a unique experience where strategy and skill will determine the winners. CAN YOU C?

Join us for Can You C?, an engaging coding event where participants will debug pre-written code and run provided test cases. This challenge is designed to test your analytical skills and attention to detail as you identify and fix errors in the setup. The event promises a competitive atmosphere, encouraging participants to showcase their coding prowess. With prizes for top performers, it's an excellent opportunity for coders to demonstrate their debugging skills and learn from one another. Don't miss this chance to be part of an exciting coding experience!
